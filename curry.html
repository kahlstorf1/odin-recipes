Red Chicken Curry

For Curry:
    2 pounds chicken breast, chunked
    1 onion, diced
    5 cloves garlic, diced
    4 knobs ginger (about thumb size)
    3 tbsp curry paste
    2 tsp Garam Masala
    1 cup chicken broth
    1 can coconut milk (16oz)
    Any vegetables desired (will use red pepper and zucchini here)
    Salt and Pepper to Taste
For Garnish (optional):
    Cilantro, coarsely chopped

    Green onions, diced

Directions

    1: Heat dutch oven to medium. Using spoon, add solid parts of coconut milk and let fry 30s. Add curry paste, and let fry another 2-3 minutes, or until fragrant. Add onion, let cook until softened. About 4-6 minutes. Add garlic and ginger, cook for another minute or until fragrant. Cut heat to low, add rest of coconut milk, chicken broth and Garam Masala.

    2: With curry broth lightly simmering, add chicken and set timer for 10-12 minutes depending on thickness of chunks. Add red pepper and zucchini with 6 minutes left on timer.

    3: Once done, garnish and serve over rice.

    Enjoy!
